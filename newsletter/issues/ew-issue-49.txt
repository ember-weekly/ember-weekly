* Featured

** In Defence of Frameworks: Why Ember Is Proud To Be One
------------------------------------------------------------

A keynote from Fluent 2014 by Yehuda Katz &amp; Tom Dale where they discuss what defines a framework compared to libraries (e.g Backbone) and toolsets (e.g. Angular), and how a framework helps the developer. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=jScLjUlLTLI)


** Yehuda Katz and Tom Dale interviewed at Fluent 2014
------------------------------------------------------------

After the Defence of Frameworks talk Yehuda &amp; Tom sat down for an interview with Brian McDonald to discuss their thoughts in a bit more detail. (https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VI__nGPT9kk)

* Reading
------------------------------------------------------------


** Views Over Components - An Ember Refactoring Story
------------------------------------------------------------

A look at how converting your views to be reusable components can simplify your application. (http://modeset.com/what-we-know/2014/03/13/views-over-components)


** Meet Ember.js: Not Just Another JS Framework
------------------------------------------------------------

Nice slides introducing Ember and its concepts. By Miguel Camba at BarcelonaJS. (http://miguelcamba.com/talks/meet-ember-js/)


** Client-side Auth with Ember.js
------------------------------------------------------------

Slides that walkthrough authenticating users with Facebook and Windows Live Oauth by using dependency injection, dependency lookup, promises, and routing hooks in Ember. (http://www.slideshare.net/mixonic/client-side-auth-with-ember)


** Building an App with Ember App Kit - Part 1
------------------------------------------------------------

First in a series of posts walking through building a simple CRUD app with Ember, Ember Data, and Ember App Kit &amp; Rails API. (http://blog.jasonkriss.com/building-an-app-with-ember-app-kit-part-1/)


** Computed Property for All Keys of an Object
------------------------------------------------------------

How to create a computed property that recomputes if any of an objects properties change by dynamically evaluating an objects properties. (http://www.thesoftwaresimpleton.com/blog/2014/03/11/cp-all-keys/)


** Building a Mobile-friendly Web App Using Ember.js and jQuery Mobile
------------------------------------------------------------

A walkthrough of how to make jQuery Mobile play nice with Ember. (http://blog.mist.io/post/78757774060/building-a-mobile-friendly-web-app-using-ember-js-and)


** [Ember.js] Implement Autoscrolling in a Chat Room
------------------------------------------------------------

An small example of using &#x60;&#x60;Ember.View&#x60;&#x60; to handle autoscrolling in a chat application. (https://medium.com/p/4028dd719511)

* Watch/Listen
------------------------------------------------------------


** HTMLBars, the future of templating in Ember.js
------------------------------------------------------------

Erik Bryn introduces HTMLBars to the Ember NYC meetup. He discusses the future of the Ember view layer including performance improvements, animation support and server-side rendering for improved SEO support. (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vjBs07kgjq0#t=3586)


** Ember Controller versus ObjectController
------------------------------------------------------------

Cory Forsyth discusses the difference between a standard &#x60;&#x60;Ember.Controller&#x60;&#x60; and an &#x60;&#x60;Ember.ObjectController&#x60;&#x60;. (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vjBs07kgjq0#t=567)


** Building Vine.co
------------------------------------------------------------

Janessa Det shows how she built the Vine.co website using Ember at Ember NYC. (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vjBs07kgjq0#t=1805)


** Ember Sparks 2: Switching to Ember Data
------------------------------------------------------------

A short example of how to use Ember Data in your application using the bloggr client to demonstrate. (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=i1Qzaf6lV1Q&amp;list=UUJasK7cAgRz1RGMJCoRLXXQ)


** NBCNews.com at Seattle Ember Meetup
------------------------------------------------------------

Lance Harper shows how his team put together the new Ember version of NBCNews.com at the Seattle meetup. (http://www.youtube.com/watch?v=4ixw_5keMTc)


** Using Persistent Data Structures with Ember.js by Jamie White 
------------------------------------------------------------

Jaime explores how using immutable data trees in Ember could work, and the advantages that it could bring. (http://vimeo.com/89089876)


** Architecting for the Enterprise by Thomas Cowell
------------------------------------------------------------

A talk from the Ember London User Group discussing when a single-page application, like those built with Ember, are the right and wrong solution. (http://vimeo.com/89076477)


** Ghostalk Episode 3 - Ember with Taras Mankovski
------------------------------------------------------------

A podcast about the Ghost open source blogging platform, which recently decided to migrate their admin UI to Ember. This episode has special guest Taras Mankovski aka EmberSherpa. (http://talk.ghost.io/3/)
