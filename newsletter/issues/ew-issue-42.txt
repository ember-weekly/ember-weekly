* Code
------------------------------------------------------------


** Ember.SimpleAuth v0.1.0 released
------------------------------------------------------------

In a major step towards v1 stability this latest version brings significant changes, including the extraction of everything specific to specific authentication/authorization mechanisms (e.g. the default OAuth 2.0 implementation) into strategy classes. (http://log.simplabs.com/post/73940085063/ember-simpleauth-0-1-0)


** ic-ajax v0.3.0 released
------------------------------------------------------------

An Ember-friendly jQuery.ajax wrapper. In this release it gains promise labels for easier debugging in the upcoming the Ember Inspector. (https://github.com/instructure/ic-ajax)
