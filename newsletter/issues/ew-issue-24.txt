** Ember 1.1.0 Beta 1 Released
------------------------------------------------------------

As promised by the [new release process](http://emberjs.com/blog/2013/09/06/new-ember-release-process.html), this week saw the first beta of Ember 1.1 released consisting mainly of bug fixes. (http://emberjs.com/builds/#/beta/latest)


** Ember Cookbooks
------------------------------------------------------------

The Ember guides now have a &quot;Cookbook&quot; section that provides answers and solutions to common Ember questions and problems. (http://emberjs.com/guides/cookbook/)

** New Core Team Member: Alex Matchneer
------------------------------------------------------------

Congratulations to Alex Matchneer (@machty) aka &quot;Mr Router&quot; for being named an Ember.js Core Team Member last week. Alex is best know for his work on the Async-Router facelift, [Emblem.js](http://emblemjs.com/) &amp; [Embercasts](http://www.embercasts.com/) (with Erik Bryn). (http://emberjs.com/team/)
