* Releases
------------------------------------------------------------


** Ember 1.2.0 released
------------------------------------------------------------

Components and helpers registered on the container can be rendered in templates via their dasherized names, non-block form link-to helper, support for nested loading/error substates, mainly bug fixes and Handlebars v1.1 support [changelog](https://github.com/emberjs/ember.js/blob/v1.2.0/CHANGELOG) (http://emberjs.com/builds/#/release/latest)


** Ember 1.3.0 Beta 1 Available
------------------------------------------------------------

Currently mainly bug fixes and Ember Testing improvements. ([changelog](https://github.com/emberjs/ember.js/blob/v1.3.0-beta.1/CHANGELOG.md#ember-130beta1)) (http://emberjs.com/builds/#/beta/latest)


** Ember Inspector fo Firefox
------------------------------------------------------------

The popular tool for debugging your Ember applications is also available on Firefox [as we as Chrome](https://chrome.google.com/webstore/detail/ember-inspector/bmdblncegkenkacieihfhpjfppoconhi?hl=en). (https://addons.mozilla.org/en-US/firefox/addon/ember-inspector/)

** Ember App Kit
------------------------------------------------------------

Ember App Kit aims to be the foundation for ambitious web applications built with Ember. Includes, a sane project structure, ES6 module support, project compilation &amp; minification, testing, linting &amp; preprocessor support. (https://github.com/stefanpenner/ember-app-kit)
