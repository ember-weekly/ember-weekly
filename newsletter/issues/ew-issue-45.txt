** Run Loop Guide
------------------------------------------------------------

Ember now has official guide documentation on the mystical run-loop, thanks to Brendan Briggs. (http://emberjs.com/guides/understanding-ember/run-loop/)


** Testing Ember Apps With Venus
------------------------------------------------------------

Chad Hietala looks at writing Ember tests using Mocha, Venus test runner and Ember&#x27;s container for dependency injection. (http://chadhietala.com/emberjs/2014/02/09/Testing-Ember-Apps-With-Venus.html)


** Custom Ember Computed Properties
------------------------------------------------------------

A guide to creating your own &#x60;&#x60;Em.computed&#x60;&#x60; property. (http://robots.thoughtbot.com/custom-ember-computed-properties)


** Structure of an Ember app
------------------------------------------------------------

A diagram illustrating the structure of an Ember app. (http://samhuri.net/blog/2014.02.03-ember-structure)


** Using Ember’s PromiseProxyMixin with ArrayController
------------------------------------------------------------

A nice summary of using &#x60;&#x60;Ember.PromiseProxyMixin&#x60;&#x60; to automatically set a controller’s content to the resolved value of the promise. (http://nragaz.com/post/60567688560/using-embers-promiseproxymixin-with-arraycontroller)


** 2-Way Data Binding under the Microscope
------------------------------------------------------------

A close look at what 2-way binding gives you, and how it differs in 3 implementations: Angular, Ember and Joosy. (http://staal.io/blog/2014/02/05/2-way-data-binding-under-the-microscope/)


** Extinguish the Fire with Ember.js
